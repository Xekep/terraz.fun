                                  </ol>

                                    </li>
                                  </ol>

                                        To use commands WorldEdit you need to activate NumLock, because most of commands are on Numpad.</p> <p>
                                          <strong>Select an area to work</strong>
                                        </p>
                                        
                                        <ol>
                                          <li>
                                            Place your mouse on the first point, and press Z.
                                          </li>
                                          <li>
                                            Hold Z, and move your mouse on the second point.
                                          </li>
                                          <li>
                                            Release Z.
                                          </li>
                                        </ol>
                                        
                                        <p>
                                          <strong>Chose a wall/block/colour of a tile</strong>
                                        </p>
                                        
                                        <ol>
                                          <li>
                                            Place your mouse on the tile.
                                          </li>
                                          <li>
                                            Press Z, and release. The ID of the wall/block/tile will appear in the chat
                                          </li>
                                        </ol>
                                        
                                        <p>
                                          <strong>Replace a tile</strong>
                                        </p>
                                        
                                        <ol>
                                          <li>
                                            Place your mouse on the first tile to replace, and press Z.
                                          </li>
                                          <li>
                                            Place your mouse on the second tile to put in the place of the fist one, and press Z.
                                          </li>
                                          <li>
                                            Select an area to replace a tile.
                                          </li>
                                          <li>
                                            Press NumPad_SLASH to complete the replacing.
                                          </li>
                                        </ol>
                                        
                                        <p>
                                          <strong>Copy buildings from solo game, or from extern server</strong>
                                        </p>
                                        
                                        <ol>
                                          <li>
                                            Select the area to copy (To know how, please check &#171;Copy buildings from the server into your world&#187;).
                                          </li>
                                          <li>
                                            Create a scheme Right Click+S.
                                          </li>
                                          <li>
                                            Login to server with the same nickname.
                                          </li>
                                          <li>
                                            Load the scheme into buffer by pressing Right CTRL+D.
                                          </li>
                                          <li>
                                            Select the upper left corner, and press Z.
                                          </li>
                                          <li>
                                            Paste the area from buffer by pressing NumPad_PLUS
                                          </li>
                                        </ol>
